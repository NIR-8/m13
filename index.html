How to use

Step 1: Enter Std Qty (per-lot) — this field is mandatory.
Step 2: Choose number of lots (1–5). Only selected lots will be visible.
Step 3: Std Qty will be copied to each visible Lot A. Enter Issued (B) and C values.
Step 4: Optional: enter a manual Total C and click "Distribute C" to split it equally among visible lots.
